# PeerNet

A one-stop web app for all university and college students to connect with their peers, and share their knowledge.
## Components

This application contains 2 components:

### Available Peers
In this section, the available seniors are displayed, alongwith the subjects they can offer help in and their email.  
You can also filter the available peers on the basis of subject.  
On clicking the chat option next to an available peer you will be redirected to WhatsApp chat with the selected peer.

### Enlist
This form offers an option for students to enlist and offer their guidance to others. This form will also authenticate the student's registration number with the university's data.

## Deployment

This application is deployed at https://peernet.netlify.app/
